About this archive

यो कि छ?

This is a living archive of Kumaoni history, culture, and everyday practice — built so the next generation of Kumaonis, in the hills, in Indian metros, or in the diaspora, can not just read their heritage but live it: say the words, cook the food, keep the festivals.

Every claim is sourced

Entries carry per-claim confidence labels — verified (two independent sources), attested (one source), unverified — and a full source list. Where traditions vary between villages or families, we record the variation; we never declare one version "correct".

This phase is internet-sourced

Online sources on Kumaoni lived practice are thin and often confuse Kumaon with Garhwal — so every entry is labeled pending community verification. The next phase brings in the real authorities: elders and practitioners, whose testimony outranks anything published here.

Help us

If your इजा or बुबु says we got something wrong — that's exactly what we want to hear. Every entry lists its open questions.
